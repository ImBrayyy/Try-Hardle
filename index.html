Try-Hardle

Welcome to Try-Hardle, a game based off of the popular New York Times "Wordle".

You have a certain amount of tries to guess the hidden word.(4-6 characters in length)

In this addtion, the words you recieve are increasingly harder and more obscure.

To make it more difficult, the color scheme is random, leaving you, the player, to figure out what each random color represents.

It should also be noted that if there is only one occurence of a letter, but you guess more than one, all of them will show, leaving it to be deceptive. (look at example if needed)

For Example:

The user is unsure if the 'A' is in the correct spot due to the random color, but they do know that it has a place in the word.

Although every occurence of the letter 'E' is highlighted, there may still only be one 'E' in the puzzle. On the other hand however, there may be more

Final warning: I can not guarentee that the words will be completely appropriate all the time and may not always supply the player with the proper and most relevant definition at the end. Play at your own risk.
